How it works

Focused prompts that build into a sharper map.

01

Start from experience

Each step starts with something concrete: a memory, a tension, a recurring pull, or a kind of work you keep coming back to.

02

Go deeper when it matters

When an answer has a useful signal inside it, LifeTask asks a focused follow-up to make the pattern easier to see.

03

Refine your fit over time

As new evidence comes in, your Lifetask map becomes more specific about your drives, strengths, frictions, and possible fit.
