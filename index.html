From confusion to clarity. Discover your LifeTask.

LifeTask guides you through focused reflection so, with your past experiences and recurring patterns, we can unravel the life path that fits you best, then use that to build your skills and learning path.

Most people live a reactionary life, guided by shallow principles that lead them into jobs or paths that never truly fit them, creating dissatisfaction and a lack of energy and motivation.

Our mission is to help you rediscover the true direction that may have been buried deep down, so we can help you rebuild a viable path that actually makes you happy and motivated.

Here is how we help.

Deep Dive

Your main working area.

Based on what you tell us in onboarding, we build a comprehensive series of sessions specifically tailored for you. Together, we find out how your past and desires shaped your personality, journey, and current future direction.

As the sessions progress, we keep using all your past conversations to further adapt your future sessions and shape your LifeTask map and Skills Board.

LifeTask Map

A continuously updating compass.

Here you will have your LifeTask map, personality overview, and specific traits. The LifeTask map is a continuously updating compass where we use what you told us to create a starting hypothesis for your life.

We take into consideration the challenges you face, the emerging direction, and the pillars guiding your actions so we can begin helping you toward the best-fit path.

The personality overview is meant to offer deeper insight into who you are, coupled with the traits we extracted so you can clearly see how they define your character.

Skills Board

Build the strengths your path needs.

The Skills Board is the place where we turn your reflection into actionable steps, so we can start building the strengths you need to adapt to your new path.

We will recommend skills we think are important based on what you told us, but you can also add, remove, or edit any skill you want, and we will take that into consideration for your learning journey.

Who it is for

For people trying to figure out what kind of work fits them best.

Students and early-career professionals exploring careers that genuinely interest them.

Professionals who feel capable, but unsure whether they are in the right role or direction.

Founders, creators, and independent workers looking to build around work they naturally enjoy.
